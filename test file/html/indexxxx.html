Our core strategy revolves around algorithmic trading, data-driven insights, and real-time decision-making, harnessing the power of machine learning and deep learning.

Join us on this exciting journey, where Quantix Capital combines data-driven strategies and artificial intelligence to unlock opportunities in the complex world of finance.

Our Service

We offer different services related to quant trading for ﬁnancial institutions, collective investment institutions and also for individuals.

Algorithmic Trading Strategy Development

Offer your skills in developing algorithmic trading strategies tailored to clients' specific requirements. Utilize your knowledge of Python, machine learning, and data analysis to create automated trading systems that can execute trades based on predefined rules.

Data Analysis and Visualization

Provide services in data analysis and visualization for financial data. Use Python and SQL to extract, clean, and analyze trading data, and then leverage Power BI to create visually appealing and insightful dashboards and reports for clients.

Help clients assess the performance of their trading strategies by conducting backtesting and performance evaluation. Utilize Python to simulate historical trades and evaluate the profitability and risk metrics of different strategies.

Trading Automation and Execution

Assist clients in automating their trading processes. Develop Python scripts or software applications that can connect to trading platforms or APIs to automatically execute trades based on predefined conditions and strategies.
